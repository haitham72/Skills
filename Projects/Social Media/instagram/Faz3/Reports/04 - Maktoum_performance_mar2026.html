CMO Advisory · Content Strategy
Strategic Recommendations for @maktoum_mrm
R1 — Redesign Financial Data Posts as Infographics
Budget launch (599 likes) and DIFC data (888 likes) underperformed because the visual was a text screenshot. The GFCI ranking post — which used a branded infographic — got 3× more likes. Immediate action: create a visual design template for all financial data announcements. The data is powerful; the packaging is killing it.
R2 — Replace 3 Boardroom Shots Per Month With Context Shots
The meeting happened in a boardroom — photograph it outside too, or in a corridor walk-and-talk, or post-meeting in natural light. The Richemont CEO meeting (DTkUk9zE8nK) and Waymo CEO (DUV1Qtpk_PL) both show how "softer" meeting setups drive 25–40% more engagement than identical content in formal conference rooms.
R3 — Introduce Carousel Posts for Complex Financial News
Instagram carousels allow a visual hook on slide 1 and data depth on slides 2–5. The Federal Budget post could be: Slide 1 = "AED 92.4B — People First" visual, Slide 2 = Public Services allocation, Slide 3 = Education, Slide 4 = D33 context. This drives Saves and Shares from the financial community — the most valuable metric for his audience.
R4 — Double Down on National Recognition Posts
Honoring Khalid Al Bustani (6,631) and Mohammad Al Gergawi (4,941) both massively outperformed. The Emirati professional class rewards public recognition of national service heroes. Identify upcoming retirements, promotions, and milestones in the financial ecosystem and post tributes proactively — these are guaranteed engagement anchors.
R5 — Activate the "Leadership Proximity" Trigger Deliberately
The UAE President meeting post (12,641 likes) wasn't lucky — it's a repeatable trigger. Every meeting with HH MBZ, HH MBR, or Crown Prince should be documented and published same-day. Warm outdoor two-shot format consistently outperforms formal indoor settings. This is his single highest-leverage content action.
